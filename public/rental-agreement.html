2. Driver Requirements

Renter and any authorized driver must:

  • Be at least 21 years of age
  • Possess a valid government-issued driver's license
  • Provide identification prior to vehicle release

Only drivers approved by SLY Transportation Services may operate the vehicle.

3. Rental Period

Rental period begins and ends at the times specified in the booking. Vehicle must be returned to the agreed location at the scheduled return time.

Late Return: A 2-hour grace period is provided.

After the grace period:

  • Up to 4 hours late: $50 late fee
  • More than 4 hours late: renter will be charged an additional full day rental rate

Continued failure to return the vehicle may result in additional charges, recovery action, and reporting of the vehicle as unlawfully withheld where permitted by law.

4. Vehicle Use Restrictions

Renter agrees NOT to:

  • Allow unauthorized drivers

Violation may result in termination without refund.

5. Mileage & Geographic Restrictions

Mileage is unlimited within Los Angeles County or within a 50-mile radius. Travel outside this area requires prior written approval.

Unauthorized travel may result in:

  • $500 penalty
  • Immediate termination
  • No refund

6. GPS Tracking Disclosure

Vehicle is equipped with GPS tracking for:

Tracking is not used for continuous personal surveillance beyond enforcement of rental terms. By renting, renter consents to this use.

7. Fuel Policy

Vehicle must be returned with the same fuel level. Fuel shortages will be charged at $5 per gallon.

8. Security Deposit

It will be released after the vehicle is returned and inspected with no issues (typically within 5–7 business days). The deposit may be fully or partially retained to cover damages, loss of use, cleaning, tolls, or fuel.

Economy cars (Camry): No security deposit is required.

If renter does not carry valid insurance, renter assumes full personal financial responsibility for all damages and liabilities.

10. Damage Protection Plan (Optional)

The Damage Protection Plan ("DPP") is an optional add-on that limits the renter's financial responsibility for damage to the rented vehicle ONLY. The DPP is NOT insurance and does not provide liability coverage.

Protection Plan Options:

  • Basic Protection — $15/day: Limits responsibility up to $2,500
  • Standard Protection — $30/day: Limits responsibility up to $1,000
  • Premium Protection — $50/day: Limits responsibility up to $500
  • Decline Protection: Renter is fully responsible for all damages

Important Coverage Limitation: The DPP applies ONLY to the rented vehicle. It does NOT cover other vehicles, property damage, injuries or death, or any third-party liability. Renter remains fully responsible for all third-party claims.

How Charges Are Applied: Protection cost = daily rate × rental days. Added at checkout.

Eligibility: SLY Transportation Services reserves the right to require a protection plan or deny rental if insurance is insufficient.

Exclusions (Coverage Void If):

  • Unauthorized drivers
  • Violation of agreement terms
  • Off-road or prohibited use
  • Intentional damage
  • Failure to report damage within 24 hours

If any of the above occur, renter is fully responsible for all damages.
