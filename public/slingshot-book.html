LA SLINGSHOT RENTALS — VEHICLE RENTAL AGREEMENT

PARTIES

Company: LA Slingshot Rentals — (844) 511-4059 — info@slytrans.com

Renter: Name, phone, email, and driver's license number as provided at time of booking.

VEHICLE INFORMATION

Vehicle: Polaris Slingshot. Vehicle is equipped with GPS tracking via Bouncie for theft prevention, location monitoring, and driving behavior. Tampering with the tracking device is strictly prohibited.

RENTAL PERIOD

Rental start and end times are as specified at the time of booking. The vehicle must be returned to the same location at the agreed return time.

Late Fee: $100/hour after a 30-minute grace period. After 3 hours late, a full additional package charge applies.

DRIVER ELIGIBILITY

Renter must be 21 years of age or older with a valid driver's license. Only the approved renter may operate the vehicle.

SECURITY DEPOSIT (Refundable)

A $500 refundable security deposit is required at time of booking. It will be returned after the vehicle is inspected upon return (typically within 5–7 business days). Deposit covers damages, late fees, cleaning, traffic violations, tolls, and fuel.

USE RESTRICTIONS

Renter agrees NOT to:

  • Drive under the influence of drugs or alcohol
  • Allow unauthorized drivers to operate the vehicle
  • Use the vehicle for racing, drifting, or reckless driving
  • Leave the state of California without prior written approval
  • Use for commercial or rideshare purposes
  • Smoke or allow pets in the vehicle
  • Operate the vehicle off-road

DAMAGE & LIABILITY

Renter accepts full responsibility for any damage during the rental period, including mechanical damage caused by misuse, tire, wheel, or interior damage. Estimated damage liability cap: $1,000.

If damage occurs, renter will be charged immediately using the payment method on file. Additional charges may be invoiced if damages exceed the deposit.

ACCIDENT PROCEDURE

In case of accident, renter must: (1) notify the company immediately at (844) 511-4059, (2) file a police report, and (3) send photos to info@slytrans.com. Failure to report may result in full liability.

FUEL POLICY

The vehicle must be returned with the same fuel level as at pickup (Full). Any deficiency will be charged at $5/gallon.

CLEANING POLICY

Standard cleaning is included with every rental. Additional fees apply for smoking ($250), excessive dirt or contamination ($100), or interior damage.

TRAFFIC VIOLATIONS

Renter is responsible for all tickets, tolls, and parking violations incurred during the rental. An admin processing fee of $25 per violation applies.

CANCELLATION POLICY

Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before pickup. Late cancellation fees are non-refundable. No-show policy: no refund — full charge applies.

PAYMENT AUTHORIZATION & CHARGEBACK POLICY

By signing this agreement, renter expressly authorizes LA Slingshot Rentals to charge the payment method on file for all amounts owed, including rental charges, security deposit deductions, vehicle damage, repair costs, late fees, fuel, cleaning, smoking, towing, storage, tickets, tolls, and administrative fees.

Renter agrees not to dispute, reverse, or initiate a chargeback for any legitimate charge incurred in accordance with this agreement. In the event of a payment dispute, this signed agreement serves as binding proof of authorization.

GOVERNING LAW

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California. Disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Los Angeles County. By signing, the renter acknowledges they have read, understood, and agreed to all terms above.
