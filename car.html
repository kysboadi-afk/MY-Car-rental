SLY TRANSPORTATION SERVICES — VEHICLE RENTAL AGREEMENT

1. RENTAL TERMS

The renter agrees to rent the vehicle described above for the specified rental period. The vehicle must be returned to the same location at the agreed return date and time. Late returns may incur additional charges.

The renter must be at least 21 years of age and hold a valid driver's license. Only the renter and pre-approved additional drivers listed by SLY Transportation may operate the vehicle.

3. CONDITION & CARE

The renter accepts the vehicle in its current condition and agrees to return it in the same condition, normal wear and tear excepted. Any damage, loss, or theft that occurs during the rental period is the renter's responsibility.

4. PROHIBITED USES

The renter agrees NOT to: use the vehicle for any illegal purpose; transport more passengers than the vehicle is designed to carry; tow or push any trailer or vehicle; drive off-road or on unpaved surfaces; smoke inside the vehicle; or allow an unauthorized driver to operate the vehicle.

5. FUEL POLICY

The vehicle will be provided with a full tank of fuel. The renter must return the vehicle with a full tank. Failure to do so will result in a refuelling charge at the prevailing local rate plus a service fee.

6. INSURANCE & LIABILITY

The renter is responsible for any damage to the vehicle not covered by SLY Transportation's insurance policy. The renter's personal auto insurance or credit card coverage may apply; it is the renter's responsibility to verify this before the rental begins.

7. SECURITY DEPOSIT

A security deposit (where applicable) is charged at the time of booking and will be refunded after the vehicle is returned in satisfactory condition, typically within 5–7 business days.
