How It Works

📋

1. Submit Your Application

Click Apply Now and fill out our quick rental application. Provide your name, phone number, age, driving experience, delivery app(s), and a copy of your driver’s license for review.

✅

2. Get Approved

Our team reviews your application and contacts you to complete the approval process. You’ll receive confirmation once you’re approved and ready to book.

🚗

3. Choose Your Car & Book Dates

Once approved, browse our available vehicles, pick the one that fits your needs, and select your pickup and return dates.

✍️

4. Sign the Agreement

Review and e-sign your rental agreement online — no printing, no waiting, no back-and-forth.

💳

5. Pay & Drive

Complete your secure payment — then hit the road!

🛣️

Unlimited Mileage

Drive as much as you need without worrying about mileage limits. Our rentals include unlimited mileage so you can focus on your trips, rideshare work, or daily driving with complete flexibility.

🛡️

Insurance Requirements

All of our vehicles are fully insured. However, renters are required to have their own insurance coverage to protect themselves in case of an accident. You may use your personal auto insurance if it meets the required coverage, or you can contact us to arrange insurance coverage when renting the vehicle.
